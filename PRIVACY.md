# Privacy Policy for Kurbot Friend

**Last Updated:** April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, and shared when you interact with the `kurbot-friend` Reddit app.

## 1. Information We Collect
When you interact with `kurbot-friend` on Reddit, the app processes the text content you submit in the chat experience. We do not intentionally collect Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your real name, email address, or IP address.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
The text content the app reads is used strictly for one purpose: generating a relevant response.

To achieve this, the app transmits the text to a third-party artificial intelligence service, **Google Gemini** (generativelanguage.googleapis.com). Gemini processes this text to generate the app's reply.

## 3. Data Storage and Retention
The app stores chat messages in Reddit Devvit Redis so conversation history can be shown again for the same Reddit post. This storage is used only to support the app experience.

Please note that data sent to Google Gemini is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell or rent your data. We share submitted chat text with Google Gemini only as needed to generate AI responses.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact the developer on Reddit at u/Significant_Rip_4961 or via GitHub at https://github.com/Dtzul04.
