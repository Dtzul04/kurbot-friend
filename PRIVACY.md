# Privacy Policy for kurbot-friend

**Last Updated:** April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, and shared when you interact with the `kurbot-friend` Reddit bot.

## 1. Information We Collect
When you invoke or interact with `kurbot-friend` on Reddit, the bot processes the text content of the specific comment or post it was called to respond to. We do not intentionally collect or store Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your real name, email address, or IP address.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
The text content the bot reads is used strictly for one purpose: generating a relevant response. 

To achieve this, the bot transmits the text to a third-party artificial intelligence service, **Groq** (api.groq.com). Groq processes this text to generate the bot's reply. 

## 3. Data Storage and Retention
We do not maintain our own databases to log or store your Reddit comments, usernames, or the generated responses. Once the bot replies on Reddit, the interaction is complete on our end.

Please note that data sent to Groq is subject to [Groq's own Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/). 

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties other than Groq, which acts purely as our service provider to generate the AI responses.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact the developer on Reddit at u/Significant_Rip_4961 or via GitHub at https://github.com/Dtzul04.
